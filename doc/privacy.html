Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Thank you for using Swift Search ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclosure, and safeguard your information when you use our WordPress plugin.

2. Information We Collect

A. Plugin Data

Swift Search connects your WordPress site to a Typesense server. The plugin indexes your content (posts, pages, products) and sends it to your designated Typesense instance.

  • Search Queries: Search terms entered by users on your site are sent to Typesense to retrieve results.
  • Search Analytics: We store aggregated data on "Top Queries" and "No Result Queries" to help you optimize your store.
  • Indexed Content: Publicly available content on your site is indexed. We do not index private or password-protected content unless explicitly configured.

B. License Data (Freemius)

Unless you opt-out, we use Freemius SDK to handle licensing and updates. Freemius may collect:

  • Site URL and WordPress version.
  • Plugin version and active status.
  • Admin name and email (only if you opt-in to usage tracking).

3. Third-Party Services

Typesense

If you use Typesense Cloud, their privacy policy applies to the data stored on their servers. If you self-host Typesense, you retain full control over your data.

4. Data Security

We do not store your API keys on our servers. Your Typesense API keys are stored in your own WordPress database. We recommend keeping your Admin API Key secret and only exposing the Search-Only API Key to the frontend.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@loopstates.com.
